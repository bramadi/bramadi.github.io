I am very happy to receive the news that I have passed the top 10 of the Pfizer Biotech Fellowship (PBF) 2021. PBF is a fellowship and scholarship program initiated by Pfizer Indonesia and Tenggara Strategics for medical biotechnology students in Indonesia.

## Upcoming Training Sessions

I can’t wait to participate in the next stage, namely online training sessions (Virtual Mentoring and Virtual Bootcamp), held in October-November 2021. This training is designed to improve students' research skills and abilities in the field of health biotechnology.

## Acknowledgments

Finally, a big thank you to my thesis supervisors (Dr. Rarastoeti Pratiwi, M.Sc., and Dr. Putra Santoso, M.Si.), Pfizer Indonesia, Tenggara Strategics, and all those around me who have helped and provided the opportunity for me to continue learning and improving my skills.
